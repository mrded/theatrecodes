Theatre Codes

A handy app for clinicians to help with finding OPCS-4 procedure codes.

View project onGitHub

Theatre Codes provides a simple method for finding the correct OPCS-4 codes used in the NHS for the classification of interventions and procedures.

This facility has become increasingly important as surgeons are often expected to self-code cases when patients are listed for a procedure in clinic or on completion of surgery in theatre.

The OPCS-4 classification system is owned and maintained by the Health and Social Care Information Centre.
